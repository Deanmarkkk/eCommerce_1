The Mini Digital Art Store is an online platform where users can browse,
purchase, and download unique digital artworks. It is designed to be simple,
lightweight, and user-friendly, making it easy for art enthusiasts
to explore various art categories and buy high-quality digital
files instantly.

Purpose


• To provide a platform for selling and buying digital artworks.
• To give artists an easy way to showcase and sell their creations.
