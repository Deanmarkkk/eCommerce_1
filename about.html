The Mini Digital Art Store is an online platform where users can browse,
purchase, and download unique digital artworks. It is designed to be simple,
lightweight, and user-friendly, making it easy for art enthusiasts
to explore various art categories and buy high-quality digital
files instantly.

Purpose

• To provide a platform for selling and buying digital artworks.
• To give artists an easy way to showcase and sell their creations.

Key Purpose

• Art Collections: Organized by categories such as Abstract, Anime, Minimalist, and Landscapes.
• Shopping Cart System: Add, view, and manage selected artworks before checkout.
• Search & Filter: Quickly find specific artworks or browse by category.
• Digital Downloads: Purchased artworks are available in high-resolution digital format.
• Responsive Design: Works on both desktop and mobile devices.

Target User

• Digital art collectors
• Graphic designers looking for inspiration
• People who love customizable prints or wallpapers

Technology Used

• Frontend: HTML, CSS, and basic JavaScript
• Storage: Local server or cloud for digital files
